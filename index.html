Tea Swirl is a modernize Tea Store that combines Western and Eastern beverage elements. The owners of Tea Swirl are originally from Taiwan and graduated from Arizona State University. After graduating from ASU, the team decided to start a Tea shop in Tempe, Arizona. They want to bring the Taiwanese cultural to the Western Culture They came up with ideas of mixing the Western beverage elements (Soda) and Eastern beverage elements (Tea) and creates the most unique flavor of beverages.

Tea Swirl is a modernize Tea Store that combines Western and Eastern beverage elements. The owners of Tea Swirl are originally from Taiwan and graduated from Arizona State University. After graduating from ASU, the team decided to start a Tea shop in Tempe, Arizona. They want to bring the Taiwanese cultural to the Western Culture They came up with ideas of mixing the Western beverage elements (Soda) and Eastern beverage elements (Tea) and creates the most unique flavor of beverages. Tea Swirl wants to bring their customers to a whole new knowledge of Tea beverages, as well as giving the customers the best experiences.
